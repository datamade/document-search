About

This Document Search App is how the Planning and Development department of the Forest Preserves of Cook County catalogs many of its project documents. Below is a brief description of each category.

  • Books – Surveyor field notebooks.
  • Control Monument Maps – Documentation on placement of monument markers.
  • Surplus Parcels – Documentation on surplus parcels.
  • Deep Tunnels – MWRD Deep Tunnel project documentation.
  • Dossiers – Title Policies, Abstracts, Appraisals, other misc. documents.
  • Easements – Documentation on easements.
  • Flat Drawings – Drawings of projects managed by the Planning and Development Dept. These would include; buildings, trails, and parking lots.
  • Index Cards – Survey monument records.
  • Licenses – Licenses issued by the District.
  • Project Files – Planning and Development project files.
  • Rights of Way – Documentation on right of ways within District property.
  • Surveys – Surveys of property acquired by the District.
  • Titles – Titles, Deeds, Judgment Orders of property acquired by the District.
